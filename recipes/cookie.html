Chocolate Chip Cookie

Description

Delicious, classic chocolate chip cookies. Doughy, buttery, sweet, and perfect.

Let's walk through the recipe step-by-step.

Ingredients

  • flour
  • eggs
  • sugar
  • butter
  • chocolate chunks

Steps

  1. The first step in making these easy chocolate chip cookies to to combine the dry ingredients in a medium size bowl.
  2. Next, cream together butter and sugars, make sure to soften the butter early by taking it out of the fridge at least two hours before baking so itâ€™s ready to go when you need it.
  3. Once butter/sugar mixture is beaten well, add the eggs & vanilla and beat to combine.
  4. Add dry ingredients and stir until just combined. Then add the chocolate chips and beat until they are evenly distributed throughout the dough.
